# Curriculum spine

The fixed backbone the journey hangs off. Generated lessons sit inside these units so the path always has shape and direction. A1 through A2, ordered by everyday usefulness for someone learning to talk with family.

Each unit lists its domain and a rough core vocabulary size. Generation fills the lessons; the spine fixes the order and the targets.

## A1

1. First words. Domain greetings. Hello, goodbye, please, thank you, yes, no. Core 15.
2. Meeting people. Domain introductions. My name is, what is your name, nice to meet you, this is. Core 18.
3. How are you. Domain small talk. How are you, fine, not bad, and you, see you later. Core 15.
4. Numbers and money. Domain market. Zero to twenty, hundred, rouble, how much, expensive, cheap. Core 25.
5. At the market. Domain market. I want, I would like, this, that, a kilo of, do you have. Core 22.
6. Food and drink. Domain ordering. Coffee, tea, water, bread, large, small, with, without. Core 24.
7. Getting around. Domain directions. Where is, left, right, straight on, metro, near, far. Core 22.
8. Family. Domain family. Mother, father, brother, sister, wife, husband, child, my, your. Core 24.

## A2

9. Time and days. Domain time. Days of the week, today, tomorrow, morning, evening, at what time. Core 26.
10. Likes and wants. Domain preferences. I like, I do not like, I want to, I can, I must. Core 22.
11. Past and future, lightly. Domain everyday. Was, will be, yesterday, will go, did. Core 24.
12. Around the home. Domain home. Room, kitchen, table, to live, to work, at home. Core 24.
13. Feeling and health. Domain wellbeing. I feel, tired, ill, better, doctor. Core 22.
14. Plans and arranging. Domain social. Let us, when, where shall we, free, busy. Core 24.

## Notes

- Stress and vowel reduction get attention from unit 1, since the learner needs the open stressed o early.
- The six cases are introduced gradually through use, with explicit notes, not as a grammar block. Genitive lands around units 4 and 5 through prices and quantities.
- Cyrillic reactivation runs alongside units 1 to 3, fading transliteration as it lands.
- Domains map to the unit.domain column in the schema so the dashboard can show confidence by domain.
