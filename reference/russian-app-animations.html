Sixteen interaction feedbacks, all looping. The set covers tap and input, right and wrong, and reveal and transition, so every touch in the app has a designed response. The single vermilion accent is rationed to six reward and live moments, recording, correct, score, streak, lesson done, result, while everything neutral stays ink. Wrong answers get no colour on purpose, so success feels brighter. Built CSS-only, so any of these drops straight into the build.
